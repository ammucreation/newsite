Frequently Asked Questions

Can I resize this template for different album sizes?

Yes, you can resize the template, but we recommend maintaining the same aspect ratio (15x24) to preserve the design integrity. For different sizes, you may need to adjust some elements manually.

Are the fonts included with the purchase?

The template uses free fonts that are available for download. We provide links to all fonts used in the package. Some fonts may require a commercial license if used for other purposes.

Can I use this template for multiple clients?

Yes! Once purchased, you can use this template for as many clients as you want. However, you may not redistribute or resell the template itself.
